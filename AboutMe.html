Hello, my name is Paul Juneau.  I am an ABET accredited engineer, and graduated Magna Cum Laude in 2014 with a BSE in Biomedical Engineering from Arizona State University.  Being a Biomedical Engineer, I have a passion for leveraging technology for the benefit of others.
